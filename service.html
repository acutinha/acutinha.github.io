Orientation Transition Leader

May, 2020 - August, 2020

Orientation Transition Leaders are a team of dedicated & diverse undergraduate students whose primary responsibility is to assist new students and their families with their transition to Texas A&M University. They serve as a resource, facilitators, and official representatives of the University during New Student Conference (NSC) programs. The New Student Conference is a two-day orientation program required for all new undergraduate students designed to help connect students to campus resources, academic support, current students, and to each other. Becoming an Orientation Leader is a unique honor and a rewarding opportunity for personal and professional growth.

  • Serve as a role model and a source of information regarding academics, policies and procedures, campus services, activities, building locations, and other aspects of life at TAMUG
  • Facilitate engaging and well-organized small group discussions for new students
  • Participate in Engineering Majors student panel for incoming students and their parents
  • Assist in the preparation of New Student Conference activities and materials
